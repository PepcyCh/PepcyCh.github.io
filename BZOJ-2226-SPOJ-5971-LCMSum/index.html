给定 nnn ，求

∑i=1nlcm(i,  n)\sum_{i = 1}^{n} lcm(i, \; n) i=1∑n​lcm(i,n)

题解

\begin{align} &\sum_{i = 1}^{n} lcm(i, ;\ n) \\ = &\sum_{i = 1}^{n} \frac{n i}{gcd(i, \; n)} \\ = &n \sum_{i = 1}^{n} \frac{i}{gcd(i, \; n)} \\ = &n \sum_{d | n} \frac{1}{d} \sum_{i = 1}^{n} [gcd(i, \; n) = d] i \\ = &n \sum_{d | n} \frac{1}{d} \sum_{i = 1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{d} \rfloor} [gcd(i, \; \lfloor \frac{n}{d} \rfloor) = 1] i d \\ = &n \sum_{d | n} \sum_{i = 1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{d} \rfloor} [gcd(i, \; \lfloor \frac{n}{d} \rfloor) = 1] i \\ \end{align}
